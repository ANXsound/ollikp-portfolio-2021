FAQ

How does gravity work?

The space station is rotating ever so slightly to compensate for space's lack of gravitational forces. This creates 1/18th of the gravity we're familiar with from the Earth's atmosphere.

How long is the travel time?

The journey lasts between 60 to 68 hours (about 3 days). The space ship can reach a top speed of 38.250 km/h.

Can I pay for a ticket with deferred billing?

No, the ticket is a one-time payment, and will only be handed out when completed successfully. Please note that some banks does not allow credit transactions outside of the Earth's atmosphere.

How does internet work in space?

In collaboration with Nokia, we've managed to install 3G coverage on all our facilities within the O-Zone resort.

Can I bring my own booze?

No. Atmospheric pressure changes in the luggage room will guarantee bottles and cans to explode and cause a terrible mess...
